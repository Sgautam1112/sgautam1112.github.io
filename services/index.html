SR&ED Tax Credit Consulting

Canada's SR&ED program offers generous tax incentives for businesses that invest in research and development — yet many under-claim, miss deadlines, or leave documentation too thin to survive a CRA review. If you're building software, improving products, or solving technical problems, I help you capture what you're entitled to and make claiming a repeatable annual process, not a scramble.

The outcome: R&D cash back in the business — with claim-ready documentation and no audit anxiety.

What's included

  • SR&ED eligibility assessment of your projects
  • Claim preparation & T661 filing coordination
  • Documentation & time-tracking systems that satisfy CRA
  • Eligible expenditure calculation (proxy vs. actual method)
  • CRA review & audit support
  • Provincial R&D credits and SR&ED financing guidance
